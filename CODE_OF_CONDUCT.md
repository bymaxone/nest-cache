# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

The `@bymax-one/nest-cache` project is committed to providing a welcoming,
respectful, and professional experience for everyone who participates —
contributors, maintainers, and users alike — regardless of background or
identity.

## Our standard

We adopt the **[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1** as the code of conduct for this project. All participants are
expected to uphold it in every project space (issues, pull requests,
discussions) and whenever representing the project in public.

In short: be respectful and constructive, assume good faith, and help keep this
a professional and inclusive community.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the maintainers privately at **support@bymax.one**.
All reports are reviewed promptly and confidentially, and maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy and safety of anyone who reports an issue.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and
may take any action they deem appropriate in response to behavior they consider
inappropriate, up to and including a temporary or permanent ban from the project.
The enforcement guidelines are those described in the Contributor Covenant 2.1
linked above.
